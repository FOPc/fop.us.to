About us

    FreedomOP Remastered was orginally hosted, paid for, and managed by CrafterSmith12 on the ProBoard's and ExodusHosting network for roughly a year. After roughly a year, Crafter migrated ownership from himself to Camzie99 where the server remained and regained popularity until where it was finally handed over to an unpronounced leader.

Farewell,

As poet Cam Smith once said, “...saying goodbye hurts the worst when you know it's the final word. It comes across like a curse...so now the final word on the final page of the final chapter of this narrative we made is my weak conscious whispering words through my mouth, the very words I prayed would never come out.”

Today, Friday October 9th 2015, I'm afraid I am whimpering this unforgivable word: Goodbye.

I am sorry to my friends, I am sorry to my enemies, but, it's time we transferring ownership. We're transferring to a new era, dominated by both triumph and destruction. We don't know what lies ahead, we don't know if we'll sink or swim. We only know we're sinking. We're sorry, truly.
